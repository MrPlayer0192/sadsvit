Geography of the Ukrainian musician
"SadSvit"


Bohdan Rozvadovsky, better known as SadSvit, is a Ukrainian indie pop and post-punk singer, composer, and songwriter.
SadSvit was born on May 1, 2004 in Ivano-Frankivsk.
Idea of ​​the name
The artist's pseudonym "SadSvit" consists of two words; the first word is English Sad - sad, the second word Svit, is a transliteration of Ukrainian word "world", so the pseudonym means "Sad World".

Life and creativity
At the age of 13, Bogdan got a laptop and began to master programs for creating music. Bogdan has no musical education, but he plays the guitar. At first he was a beatmaker and made beats to order, but later he realized that he wanted to create his own music.
SadSvit first mini-album, "Sumatokha," was released on December 4, 2020 in Russian.
